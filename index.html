Alva’s Pre University College started its historic journey in 1998 with the vision of moulding a better tomorrow. Today, Alva’s has not only become a household name, but also a synonym for excellence, expertise, eminence and efficiency. Alva’s P.U College has stood out to be a very special educational institution having carved a niche for itself in a short span. In the course of fulfilling its vision, the college has made remarkable achievements and emerged as one of the premier institutions of the nation.
